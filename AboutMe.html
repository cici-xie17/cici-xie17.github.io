Who am I?
Hello! I’m Cici, a self-made product designer based in Chicago. I thrive at the cross section of creativity and technology, and I’m passionate about creating solutions through collaboration and empathy. For the last 2 years, I’ve been working in a mix of digital marketing, data analysis, and freelance UX, and I’m now seeking a full time role in product design.
My degree in Behavioral Economics from Duke University has instilled in me the importance of human psychology and cognitive emotional factors in user interactions. My experience in marketing has taught me effective communication, whether with teammates, clients, or stakeholders. Parsing through heaps of information in my current role as a data analyst has given me the technical prowess necessary to derive clarity from UX research in even the most ambiguous of cases.
When I’m not up to my eyeballs in Figma files, you can catch me hanging out on an aerial silk, framing a shot during golden hour, or trying to perfect a swing-out.
Like to know more? Flick me an email at  Cecilia.xie17@gmail.com, I’d love to chat!
